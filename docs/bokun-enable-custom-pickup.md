# Bókun — Enable custom pickup address for El Yunque

**Goal:** let customers enter their own pickup address (Airbnb, private rental, or hotel not in our list) directly during checkout on the website. Today Bókun refuses anything outside the 77 listed hotels.

**When:** anytime — the website is already coded to show or hide the "custom address" input automatically based on this Bókun setting.

**What you'll do:** flip a single toggle on the El Yunque product. Takes ~1 minute.

---

## Step 1 — Open the El Yunque product settings

1. Log in to Bókun
2. Go to **Products** (main menu)
3. Click on **Vivid El Yunque Rain Forest Day Tour with Door-to-Door Transport**
4. In the left side panel click **Pickup** (sometimes labeled "Pickup & Dropoff" or "Logistics")

## Step 2 — Enable the flag

Find the setting labeled **"Allow custom pickup"** (wording may be *"Accept pickup from non-listed locations"* depending on your Bókun version) and toggle it **ON**.

Save the change.

> ✅ That's it. Nothing else to configure.

## Step 3 — (Optional but recommended) Set a pickup zone limit

If you only want to accept custom addresses within the San Juan metro area, you can optionally:

- In the same section, look for **"Pickup zone"** or **"Accepted cities"**
- Restrict to: San Juan, Carolina, Río Grande, Luquillo

This prevents customers booking from outside your coverage (e.g. Dorado, Humacao, Bayamón).

---

## What happens after

Next time a customer books El Yunque on the website:

1. They'll first see the list of 77 listed hotels (same as today)
2. Below the list they'll see a new discreet link:

> ✎ Not staying at a listed hotel? Enter custom address

3. Clicking it opens a text field where they type their address
4. The booking confirmation email tells them your guide will call/text to confirm the exact pickup time

Your dashboard will receive these bookings with the custom address in the notes — your guide can contact the customer via WhatsApp/phone to confirm pickup details.

---

## If the flag is not available in your Bókun plan

Some Bókun plans restrict this feature. If you don't see the **"Allow custom pickup"** option:

- Option A: Contact Bókun support ([support@bokun.io](mailto:support@bokun.io)) and ask for the feature to be enabled
- Option B: Keep the current behavior (customer sees "Contact us for custom pickup" — they email micasaventuras@gmail.com directly)

The website works fine with either setting — no code change needed.

---

## Rollback

If you ever want to turn it off, just toggle the same setting back to OFF. The website will hide the custom address input automatically on next page load.
